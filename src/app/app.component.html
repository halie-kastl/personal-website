I'm a full stack application engineer with a passion for functional, beautiful things.

Development

When I began my development career I was captivated by collective attitude of the industry to never be satisfied with the status-quo, and to never rest on your laurels when you see another problem to solve. As I mature on my own path within the industry, that constant curiosity sustains my work and drives me put a growth mindset at the center of my development practice.

UX and Design

We create technology to solve human problems, and for me, effective technology absolutely requires an engaging and seamless human experience. I utilize a wide array of tools such as storyboards, user personas, and wireframes to help craft the ideal branding and UI for my projects.

Curiosity, humility, and collaboration are the values I most try to bring to my work within and without the technical space.
